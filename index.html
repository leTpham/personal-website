About

Hi, my name is Le, and I'm a prospective web developer!

As we encourage the students to to look further than their daily work, to explore their strength, to be brave and try new things, .... I saw these kids try, fail, and pick themselves up and try again; saw them slowly getting past their fear and gaining more confidence every day; saw their newfound respect for the power of resilience and curiousity.
Inspired to reflect upon the relationship I have with my own education, here I am and ready to learn.
